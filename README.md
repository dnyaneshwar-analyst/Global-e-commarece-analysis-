# Global-e-commarece-analysis-
This project is an Excel-based Global E-commerce Sales Dashboard.
It shows overall business performance using total revenue, total orders, and total quantity sold.

The dashboard helps to understand sales trends across different countries and product categories with the help of interactive slicers and charts.

Tools Used
Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts
Slicers and Timeline
Basic data cleaning and formatting

Summary
Total Revenue: 1,512,252.98
Total Orders: 1,200
Total Quantity Sold: 6,008

Insights

Category-wise Revenue
Home Decor is the highest revenue generating category with around 232K.
Books is the lowest revenue generating category with around 158K.

This shows that customers prefer Home Decor products more, while Books need better marketing or offers.

Country-wise Revenue
USA contributes the highest revenue with around 206K.
UK contributes the lowest revenue with around 110K.

USA is the strongest market for sales, whereas the UK has scope for improvement.

Quantity Sold Analysis
Home Decor has the highest quantity sold among all categories.
USA has the highest quantity sold among all countries.

Higher quantity sold generally results in higher revenue.

Dashboard Features
Category, Country, and Date filters
Revenue and Quantity comparison charts
Monthly sales trend using timeline

Business Use
This dashboard helps in identifying top and low performing categories and countries.
It can be used for better sales planning, inventory management, and business decisions.
